# Personality Videos

Place your personality videos in this folder:

- `slave.mp4` - Video to play when "slave" personality is selected
- `airdrop.mp4` - Video to play when "airdrop_farmer" personality is selected
- `vet.mp4` - Video to play when "vietnam_vet" personality is selected
- `gangster.mp4` - Video to play when "gangster" personality is selected

The videos will automatically play fullscreen when the corresponding personality is selected, then return to chat when finished.
